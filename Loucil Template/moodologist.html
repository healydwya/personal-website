Moodologist


Think of it as your personal mixologist... Moodologist is a web application that my team and I built at our very first ever hackathon, HackFSU 2015. This was my first time participating as a hacker. The idea behind this app came from a random side conversation between a few team members and I about a product we could build that would be really funny/cool to have in our college town. So, we thought of an app that would generate an alcoholic beverage based on the user's mood. Pretty funny, huh? What's even funnier (and of course really amazing) is that my team made in the Top 10 Hacks. We placed 6th for our unique project and for its well-favored creative design and user experience.

Moodologist was built on a Bootstrap framework and we developed it all in HTML, CSS, Javascrip & jQuery in under 30 hours.
