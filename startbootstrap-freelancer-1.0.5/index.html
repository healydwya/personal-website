Outdoor Explore Mobile App

At Trail Hacks, a hackathon geared toward connecting people with Tallahassee's outdoor activities, my team designed an app to integrate all of Tallahassee's outdoor events and activities into one place, as well as connect them with transit options for getting to each place. I built the app using Angular.js and Ionic, and then designed our logo using Adobe Illustrator and prepared a demonstration for our final presentation using Sketch and Adobe XD. I am proud to say that our project took home the grand prize!

  • Client: Trail Hacks
  • Date: April 2016
  • Service: Mobile App Design & Development

Rack Attack Mobile Application

This mobile app was produced as part of a group project for an IT Project Management course during my senior year. I used Angular and Ionic to build the application, and all photos and content were shot and written by our group. The purpose of the application is to promote non-motorized transit use on our university campus, and to educate students on how easy it is to ride bicycles to class.

  • Client: Florida State University
  • Date: December 2016
  • Service: Mobile App Development

Unhoused Humanity Website

Throughout my internship with Unhoused Humanity, a local startup, I updated and maintained the organization's website while managing client and donor data, creating custom forms and donation pages to further the work of the company, and brainstorming new and innovative ways to draw donors into our site.
